Infogram

Introduction

The graph displays the number of people living in a particular state in Australia.


Graph type and purpose

An treemap is used to represent the population data. Treemaps are used to simplify information compare different items to each other. The size of each rectangle boxes are in proportion to the amount of data it represents.


Data included

The population of each state are represented by the size of each rectangle boxes while different colours are used to represent identity of each state.


Main finding/trend

The main finding in the trend is that New South Wales has the largest population in Australia.


Other findings

Australian Capital Territory has the smallest population compared to the other states in the Data. New South Wales, Victoria, Queensland, Western Australia and South Australia has more than 1 million people living in them


Source of data

The source data came from Wikipedia, their website’s Address is https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Ranked_list_of_states_and_territories_of_Australia


Conclusion

Overall, the infographic shows that the population in New South Wales is the largest.
